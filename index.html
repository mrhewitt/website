Footprint Charts in Ninjatrader 8

I have been asked how I get footprint charts in Ninjatrader 8.

There are a couple of indicators for this out there, I have not tried many, but I do use the MZPack Footprint chart indicator.

This is an excellent footprint chart, and the pack comes with a number of additional orderflow indicators.

Best yet, it is a once-fee for a lifetime licence, and a very affordable one at that. I purchased just the single indicator, the Footprint, but the only reason for this is that if I purchased the entire pack I would have tried to introduce the other indicators into my trading which at the time was not a good idea (more complexity)

However, purchasing the entire pack is definity a great choice from a discount perspective, and I do plan on trying some of the other indicators for absorbation etc at some point
